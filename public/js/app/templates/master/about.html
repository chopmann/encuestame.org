About Us

Encuestame it's a free open source project, created at 2008 in Nicaragua, now our headquarters it's in Austria. This project it's free of limitations, secondary services and with Apache License v2.0.

This project it's a tool to make different type of surveys, easy to manage and distribute, with several features to share in social networks, manage content and security.

The project it's open to new contributors, ideas and tips.
