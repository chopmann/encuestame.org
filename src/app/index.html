About Us

History

Encuestame is a free open source project created in 2008 in Nicaragua and Spain . Now our headquarters are in Austria. We decided to create a RESTFul API to integrate the new User Interface with three party applications. In 2011 we received Atlassian Open Source Licenses, helping our project to become more mature.

RoadMap

This project is alive, we are working hard every day to offer you a more stable product. Everything related to our roadmap is available in this page.

Encuestame doesn't have incomes and is a non-profit and free project. We only accept donations for the maintenance of the infrastructure. Your best contribution for us is your own code.
